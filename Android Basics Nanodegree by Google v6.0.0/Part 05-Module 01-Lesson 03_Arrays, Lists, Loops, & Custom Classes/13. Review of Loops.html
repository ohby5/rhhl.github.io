13. Review of Loops

Review of Loops

You were just introduced to two types of Loops - while loops and for loops.
Then practiced using loops to display TextViews in a Layout.

As you continue developing, you'll notice loops used in a number of circumstances from Runnables (e.g. running different tasks on different tasks which will be covered later) to Image Processing. Don't worry about these implementations for now. Focus on understanding how and when to setup these types of loops and why you'd choose each type.
