03. Navigation Patterns in Other Apps

Navigation Patterns in Other Apps.

Evaluate the navigation pattern in another app.

Take some time to evaluate the navigation pattern in another app! Doing this will give you a better understanding of how apps are designed and how you can work to make better apps in the future. To do so, follow the steps below:

  1. Pick an app to download from Google Play. If you need help deciding which one to download, you can check out
  • http://androidniceties.tumblr.com/
  • https://pttrns.com/android-patterns?srtby=popularity_desc

  1. Play around with the app. Navigate through the different screens and try out the different features.

  2. For those in the Android Basics Nanodegree program, on the discussion forum, share a short description of how you navigate around the app. Take a moment to read some of the descriptions other students have left as well!
